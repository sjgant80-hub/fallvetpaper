## For the end user

Generate, preview, and issue UK veterinary clinical documents from 10 templates. Locked regulatory clauses preserve statutory and RCVS-mandated wording. Editable sections are the clinician's responsibility.

### Key rules

- **Veterinary Surgeons Act 1966**: only MRCVS may diagnose, treat, perform surgery
- **RCVS Code of Professional Conduct**: informed consent, record keeping, referral rights
- **Misuse of Drugs Regulations 2001**: Schedule 2 CD register, safe custody, running balance
- **Animal Welfare Act 2006**: duty of care, humane euthanasia
- **Fraud Act 2006**: insurance claim accuracy
